Announcements

12.23.2020 / Athletic Club Closed During Construction

As of December 23rd, the gym will be temporarily closed for redevelopment. It is scheduled to reopen Spring 2020.

The Club, which has been the home for recreation and fitness within the Galleria complex for thirty years, will undergo a complete transformation. Below are some of the exciting improvements that you can look forward to:

  • • State-of-the-art health club finishes
  • • New free weights and machines
  • • New cardio equipment, including Peloton bikes
  • • New locker rooms and showers
